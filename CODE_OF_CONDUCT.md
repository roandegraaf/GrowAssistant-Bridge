# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a positive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Other unprofessional conduct

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
